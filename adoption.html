ADOPTION

If you are interested in offering a home to any of the animals in our care please complete an adoption application form and a volunteer will be in touch to arrange an informal home visit. If for any reason you need to withdraw your application, a change of mind or you've adopted elsewhere, we would ask you to notify us as soon as possible.

CATS

Randy

Randy is a fantastic lad looking for his own second chance. He has bags of character and will make a wonderful addition to his new home. He would be ideally suited to an adopter who is looking for a cat who can be in & outdoors. He would be in his element in the country side, well off main roads with lots of adventures ahead of him. He is in good health, is now neutered and will soon be ready to start his new life. He adores interaction & lots of love, this big boy has an adorable nature. He can be gradually introduced to other cats on his new home, ideally with a female for company. Can you offer our lovely boy Randy the loving home he deserves? Background; Randy was found during the recent snow storms in a collapsed state, he had been in a few wars but due to the adverse weather conditions he was in serious risk of losing his life. He was being monitored by a caring local lady who couldn't catch him, thankfully he was caught in time and has since made a full recovery. He has been fully vet assessed and cleared for adoption. He is currently located in Donegal and if you would like to offer him a home please contact on 089 1234567 or email info@lasthope.ie for more details.
